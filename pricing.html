Frequently Asked Questions

What is an "account" in your pricing?
An "account" represents a separate research project or client. For example, if you manage 3 different Instagram accounts for different clients, you would need the Professional plan (2-49 accounts).
Can I change plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.
What's the difference between Free and paid plans?
The Free plan lets you try our viral detection technology with basic features. Paid plans unlock data export, storage, advanced analytics, and community access.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment in full.
How does the Chrome extension work?
Our Chrome extension automatically detects viral reels as you browse Instagram. It works in real-time and requires no manual input from you.
